[ note ] Case note · 2026-07-17

07 Sound Solution: a 14 KB credential that loads in under a second.

Adolfo "Fito" Martinez is a live sound engineer (FOH) based in Miami and Willy Chirino's lead engineer. He needed a press kit: the professional credential you send a promoter over WhatsApp, and it has to open instantly, on any phone, no excuses. This is what we built and why.

[01] The decision

A single page. And it's not a contradiction.

At Index01 we stand for "doors, not walls": a separate page for each thing you offer. Here we built exactly one. Architecture isn't a recipe, it's decided by reading the business: a press kit answers a single question, who he is and what he does. One question, one door. Multiplying pages would have meant inventing structure for what is, in essence, a professional identity document.

[02] The numbers

Measured, not estimated.

14 KB

Total transfer

The whole site, styles and behavior included, transfers about 14 kilobytes. Less than a single photo sent over WhatsApp. On mobile data, that's the difference between opening right away and staring at a blank screen.

< 1 s

Full load

In our cold-load measurement, the page finishes loading in under a second, with only 3 resources: the HTML, one stylesheet, and one script. No CMS, no plugins, nothing that expires or breaks on its own.

2 languages

In the same HTML

Spanish and English live on the same page and switching is instant, without reloading. A promoter in Miami and another in New York read the same credential, each in their own language.

[ + ] The layer you don't see

Under the surface, the page carries complete structured data: the person, the professional service with its geolocation, and a catalog of five services, plus an llms.txt file that explains the site to AI assistants. When someone (or an AI) asks who does Willy Chirino's sound, there's a clean source to cite. And not a single tracker: a press kit doesn't need cookies, it needs respect for whoever opens it.

[03] What it means

A site that works as a credential.

This site doesn't compete for mass searches: it works as a business card that's always ready and as a citable source on Fito's track record. You can check it yourself at 07soundsolution.com: open it on your phone and count the seconds.
