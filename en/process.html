[ process ] How we work

Clear from day one.

Before building anything, we sign a written agreement meant to be understood, not to hide anything: what gets built, what it costs, who provides what, and when payment happens. Below is the full path, no fine print.

Diagnosis

We don't hand you a template. We read your business and design for what your business is. We define the face it needs, which services go in, which sections, and what you want someone to feel when they arrive. The scope comes out of this.

What you provide, up front

The project clock starts running when everything is in: logo (or the commission to create it), images with clear rights to use them, the copy for each section, and the core decisions. While materials are missing, the timeline pauses. Waiting time isn't working time.

Staged design

We present the design and we work with an agreed number of revision rounds within scope. Approval is staged: what you approve in writing stays approved. Reopening something already closed is a new commission, with its own quote. No surprises.

Build

This is where it becomes real. Separate pages (a door for each thing you offer), fast loading, perfect behavior on the phone, accessibility best practices, and structured data so both people and the machines that now answer for them can read you.

Measurement and launch

If your business needs it, we install measurement and remarketing with the consent notice the law requires. We review everything and the site goes live when the final payment clears, along with the handover of the files. The domain and the accounts are yours, in your name.

Keeping it alive

The initial delivery doesn't include maintenance, but we offer it separately. Because these systems reward what's fresh: a website that sits still starts losing ground on its own. Maintenance feeds that process over time.

[ ownership ] The accounts are yours

The domain, hosting, and email go in your name and on your payment method. We work with delegated access, not as the owner. When the final payment clears, the code and design of this project become yours.

[ honesty ] No empty promises

We deliver the site technically ready to be found and cited from day one. We don't guarantee a specific position or a timeframe: a new site's authority is built over time and with external signals that don't depend on the code.
