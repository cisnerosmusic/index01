[ services ] What we do

Three layers that work together.

Design, ranking, and measurement. Most people sell just one; we build them to hold each other up. A pretty site no one finds falls short, and a well-ranked one with a catalog face doesn't quite sell either.

[01] Design

Custom web design

You don't look like everyone else, so your website shouldn't either. we read your business and give it a face of its own, clean and secure, with your brand up front and none of that generic catalog feel you can spot a mile away. When someone walks in, they sense a serious business is here.

  • Custom, not a template
  • Identity up front
  • Accessibility
  • Truly responsive
[02] SEO

Technical SEO, the foundations

Underneath, your site meets what a search engine actually measures today. It loads fast, works perfectly on the phone, and is structured into separate pages: each one its own door that the search engine can read and understand, instead of an infinite-scroll wall where everything gets lost.

  • Core Web Vitals
  • Speed
  • Separate pages
  • Sitemap
[03] AEO

AEO, get the AI to cite you

This is the layer few are building yet. With structured data we explain to the machine who you are, what you do, and where you are. So when someone asks an assistant for a service like yours in your area, your name is the one that comes up as the answer, sources and all.

  • Structured data
  • Schema.org
  • llms.txt
  • Citable content
[04] Measurement

Measurement and remarketing

If your business needs it, we install the Meta and Google pixels so you know where your people come from and can reach them again. We do it right, with the consent the law requires, not hidden. That protects you, which is what many overlook and later regret.

  • Meta Pixel
  • Google
  • Legal consent
  • Attribution
[ scope ] What's not included by default

To keep everything clear: copywriting, photography sessions, the logo, translation, the online store, and monthly maintenance are quoted separately if you want them. We say it up front so there are no surprises, in the scope or on the invoice.
